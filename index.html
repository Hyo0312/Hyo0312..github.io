SKKU · Dance Major

I am a dance major at Sungkyunkwan University, drawn to the profound relationship between space and the body. My practice lives at the intersection of movement, architecture, and atmosphere.

I enjoy exploring exhibitions and cultural spaces — not merely as a visitor, but as a researcher of sensation. I am endlessly curious about how a room's light, scale, and texture can shift emotional states and inspire entirely different movements.

This curiosity drives my work: to understand how atmosphere shapes the dancer before the dancer shapes the stage.
